Reframing Potential • Designed for Every Mind • Where Brilliance Takes the Stage

We don't ask our children to fit the world. We are building a world that fits them.

Beyond The Limit is an initiative created to build meaningful platforms where children can express themselves, build confidence, and be celebrated for who they are. Through inclusive and empowering experiences, it encourages children to explore their potential and step into new opportunities. Beginning with special focus events, the initiative aims to grow into a larger movement that creates many more programmes and experiences for children in the future — a true step towards many possibilities.

Thigazhis the first step of the Beyond The Limit journey, creating opportunities for children to shine with confidence, grow with pride, and be celebrated for their uniqueness.

The Runway of Possibilities

Thigazh is created especially for neurodivergent children, including those with autism, ADHD, Down syndrome, and other developmental differences.

Thigazh is designed as an inclusive fashion walk where children are given a joyful and supportive stage to express themselves, build confidence, and experience the excitement of being celebrated. The event focuses on creating a positive environment where every child feels confident, proud, valued, and encouraged.

Through Thigazh, children are not just walking a ramp, they are stepping into a moment that celebrates their confidence, individuality, courage, and unique journey

Organizers

SRP Foundation & Shree Renga Polyester
Shree Renga Polyester Pvt Ltd, a sustainability-driven textile company based in Karur, converts post-consumer PET bottles into high-value recycled polyester products, contributing to a circular economy and responsible production — truly turning waste into value.

Extending this vision beyond manufacturing, the SRP Foundation, the CSR arm of Shree Renga Polyester, works towards social impact through initiatives in education, women empowerment, youth well-being, community infrastructure, and sustainability.

The Partnership

Brainchild Rehab Centre (BRC) and Crystal Neuro Centre bring specialised expertise in therapeutic support, developmental assessment, behavioural understanding, and family-centric care. Their structured approaches ensure clinical sensitivity, child-first ethics, sensory respect, and a safe environment where every child participates comfortably and confidently.

The Founders

K. Jayashree Senthil, Executive Director of SRPPL and Chairperson of SRP Foundation, is a social impact leader committed to bridging corporate strength with community transformation.

Dr. Vishnu L, Founder of Brainchild Rehab Centre (BRC) and a PhD Scholar in Occupational Therapy, ensures the initiative remains grounded in child-first ethics, safety, and structured therapeutic care.

Vision

To build an inclusive society where neurodivergent children and individuals of diverse abilities are recognised for their strengths, respected for their individuality, and given opportunities to shine. Beyond The Limit envisions a future where confidence replaces stigma and acceptance becomes the norm.

Mission

To create safe, structured, and empowering platforms that celebrate every child’s uniqueness, promote dignity and inclusion, provide sensory-aware and child-friendly experiences, support families through community connection, and encourage greater awareness and understanding in society.
